Ghirardelli Brownies Recipe

  • home
  • nutrition

1/4 Cup of Water
1/3 of Cup Vegetable Oil
1 Egg
Ghirardelli Brownie Mix

Preheat oven to 325Â°F.
Prepare baking pan by lightly greasing or spraying with non-stick cooking spray.
Place water, oil and egg in medium bowl.
Stir until fully mixed.
Add brownie mix and stir until well blended.
Spread in prepared pan.
Bake for 40-45 minutes.
Cool completely in pan before cutting.
Do not overbake. Fresh-baked brownies appear underbaked but cool to doneness.
For thicker brownies, make batter using 2 tablespoons of water, 1/3 cup of vegitable oil and 2 eggs.
HIGH ALTITUDE: Add 1/4 cup all-purpose flour and an additional 2 tablespoons of water.
